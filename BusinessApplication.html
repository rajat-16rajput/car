Business Application

A business application can be any Product/Application of Deluxe

People with different Business Capabilitites can have access to Various Business applications.

Consider the example of the application Workday which shows the Employee Details. So, the HR with the Business capability of Learning and Development and the HR with the Business Capability of Talent Management BOTH can access this common Business Application.
